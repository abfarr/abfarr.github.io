Hi, my name is Alexi, and I was/am trying to be again a data scientist. I have a Bachelor’s in Math and and Master’s in Data Science science.

Mathematical problem solving is my passion, which can be theoretical and lofty. Data science is the way I operationalize that passion for the real world. Finding patterns and extracting insights from noisy, messy data points is a challenge and an adventure.

My day job generally does not involve data, modeling, or machine learning. So this is my passion project: re-learning how to be a good data scientist -- from the ground up. I'm refreshing myself on what I knew, learning all the things that no one ever formally taught me and yet are critical to data science today, and exploring where the field has gone. It's going to be quite the journey.

Whatever brought you here, thanks for sharing in the trip.
